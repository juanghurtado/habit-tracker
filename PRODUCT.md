# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A single user tracking their personal daily habits. Primary context: mobile browser, quick interactions throughout the day (tapping "I did it" in under 2 seconds). Secondary: laptop for managing habits. Used in all environments — home, work, commute, outdoors, in bed at night.

## Product Purpose

A dead-simple habit tracker that gets out of your way. The core interaction is one tap: "I did it". The app exists to make habit tracking frictionless enough to sustain as a daily practice.

## Brand Personality

Playful-warm, friendly, approachable. Not corporate, not gamified, not sterile. Feels like a cozy personal tool — colourful without being childish, warm without being cloying.

## Anti-references

- No corporate/enterprise aesthetics (no navy-blue headers, muted greys, rigid tables)
- No classic SaaS patterns (no onboarding wizards, no freemium upsells, no dashboards with KPIs)
- No beige/cream/sand backgrounds (the default AI warm-neutral)
- No Apple Health minimalism (no ultra-flat greyscale, no lists of numbers and pills)

## Design Principles

1. **One tap, done** — every interaction should be completable in one motion. The "I did it" button is the hero.
2. **Mobile-first, finger-friendly** — touch targets are generous, spacing is airy, nothing is crammed.
3. **Playful warmth** — colour and rounded shapes create a friendly feeling. Motion is part of the personality.
4. **The app works for you, not the other way around** — there's no streak pressure, no gamification guilt. Just a log.
5. **Dark and light, both right** — the app respects system preference and looks good in both modes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast minimums
- `prefers-reduced-motion` respected
- Large touch targets (minimum 44px)
- System font stack for maximum legibility on all devices
